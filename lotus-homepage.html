Our Story

A century of craft.
Reborn for today.

Since 1947, LOTUS has been synonymous with precision engineering and enduring quality. Trusted by garment factories and home seamstresses alike, the LOTUS name became a hallmark of reliability across generations of craftspeople.

After years of silence, the legend returns — reimagined for a new era of makers, designers, and manufacturers who demand the finest in sewing technology.
